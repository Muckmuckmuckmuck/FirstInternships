The Best Time to Send a Cold Email for an Internship

Updated July 1, 2026

The best time to send a cold email is Tuesday to Thursday, mid-morning (around 8–10 a.m. in the recipient's time zone). Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons. That said, a great email sent at a mediocre time still beats a mediocre email sent at the perfect time.

Timing tips

  • Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 a.m. local time tends to get the best open rates.
  • Space out a batch of emails rather than blasting them all at once.
  • Content matters far more than timing — personalize first.
