How to Email a Recruiter About an Internship

Updated July 1, 2026

To email a recruiter about an internship, keep it under 120 words: introduce yourself, name one specific reason you're interested in their company, ask if they're taking interns, and attach your resume. Recruiters read short, specific emails and ignore long generic ones.

What recruiters want to see

  • A clear, plain subject line.
  • One line on who you are and what you study.
  • One specific, real reason — not flattery.
  • One direct question and an attached resume.
