2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate the Service and generate AI-drafted emails
  • Calculate company fit scores tailored to your background
  • Track your outreach to prevent duplicate sends
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

Where you have given marketing consent, we also use and share your information for marketing as described in Section 3A below.

3. The Company Database

Our company database contains business contact information used for job and internship outreach, including publicly available role-based addresses (such as careers@) and, in some cases, named contacts and their work emails. Some records are added through an AI-assisted discovery feature that uses Google's Gemini API with Google Search to find these addresses from publicly available sources.

If you are a contact in our database and want your information removed, email optout@firstinternships.com. We will remove your personal details within 5 business days.

3A. Marketing, Sharing, and Sale of Personal Information

If you opted in to marketing at signup, we may use and share the personal information associated with your account — name, contact details, profile, and outreach activity — to send you opportunities and to share, license, or sell it to vetted third-party partners, advertisers, and affiliated services for their own marketing. Under some US state privacy laws, this sharing may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.

Your choices:

  • Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: California, and residents of other states with similar rights, may direct us to stop selling or sharing their personal information by emailing optout@firstinternships.com with the subject "Do Not Sell or Share," or toggling marketing off in account settings.
  • Unsubscribe: every marketing email includes a one-click unsubscribe.
  • EEA / UK users: we rely on your opt-in consent for this sharing, and you may withdraw it at any time with no effect on the lawfulness of prior processing.

We do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of anyone under 18.

4. How We Store Your Data

Account and profile data is stored in our database (Supabase). Your email send-permission token is stored only in your browser's session storage and is cleared when you close your browser — it is never written to persistent storage outside the OAuth flow. Payments are handled by Stripe; we never see your full card number.

6. Data Retention

  • Account/profile: until you delete your account
  • Payment records: up to 7 years (tax/legal)

To delete your account and associated data, email hello@firstinternships.com. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Security

We use HTTPS for data in transit, store send-permission tokens in session storage only, and apply reasonable safeguards. No system is perfectly secure. If a breach affects your personal data, we will notify you as required by law.

8. Your Rights

Everyone

  • Access, correction, deletion, portability of your data — email privacy@firstinternships.com.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information (see Section 3A — sharing only occurs if you opted in), and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. Submit requests to privacy@firstinternships.com; we respond within 45 days.

EEA / UK (GDPR)

You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability. Our legal basis is contractual necessity and legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention). Contact privacy@firstinternships.com.

FirstInternships is operated from the United States. If you are outside the US, your data may be processed in the US, which may not provide the same level of protection as your home country.

9. Children's Privacy

FirstInternships is not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact hello@firstinternships.com and we will delete it.

10. Cookies

We use session and local storage to keep you signed in and remember preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified by email or in-app at least 14 days before taking effect.
