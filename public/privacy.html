1A. Google User Data and Limited Use

When you connect your Google account, we request only the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send scope, which lets us send email on your behalf. We cannot read, search, download, or otherwise access any message in your mailbox.

FirstInternships's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, data obtained through Google APIs — including your Gmail send authorization and anything derived from it — is used only to provide and improve the user-facing feature of sending emails you compose, is never sold, shared, transferred, or used for advertising, and is never used to train generalized AI/ML models. Humans do not read this data except where you give explicit consent, where required for security or to comply with law, or as needed for limited internal operations with data de-identified or aggregated.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate the Service and generate AI-drafted emails
  • Calculate company fit scores tailored to your background
  • Track your outreach to prevent duplicate sends
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

Where you have given marketing consent, we also use and share your information for marketing as described in Section 3A below.

3. The Company Database

Our company database contains business contact information used for job and internship outreach, including publicly available role-based addresses (such as careers@) and, in some cases, named contacts and their work emails. Some records are added through an AI-assisted discovery feature that uses Google's Gemini API with Google Search to find these addresses from publicly available sources.

If you are a contact in our database and want your information removed, email optout@firstinternships.com. We will remove your personal details within 5 business days.

3A. Marketing, Sharing, and Sale of Personal Information

If you opted in to marketing at signup, we may use and share the personal information associated with your account — name, contact details, profile, and outreach activity — to send you opportunities and to share, license, or sell it to vetted third-party partners, advertisers, and affiliated services for their own marketing. Under some US state privacy laws, this sharing may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.

This never includes data obtained through Google APIs. Your Gmail send authorization, and any data derived from it, is never sold, shared, licensed, or transferred for marketing or any other purpose — regardless of your marketing settings — and is used only to provide the Service, as described in Section 1A.

Your choices:

  • Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: California, and residents of other states with similar rights, may direct us to stop selling or sharing their personal information by emailing optout@firstinternships.com with the subject "Do Not Sell or Share," or toggling marketing off in account settings.
  • Unsubscribe: every marketing email includes a one-click unsubscribe.
  • EEA / UK users: we rely on your opt-in consent for this sharing, and you may withdraw it at any time with no effect on the lawfulness of prior processing.

We do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of anyone under 18.

4. How We Store Your Data

Account and profile data is stored in our database (Supabase). When you connect Gmail, Google issues us a send-only authorization token (a refresh token scoped to gmail.send). We store this token securely on our servers, encrypted at rest, with access restricted to the service that sends your emails. We use it solely to send messages at your explicit instruction, and never to read, store, or access your inbox. You can disconnect Gmail at any time, which revokes and deletes the token. We never receive or store your Google password. Payments are handled by Stripe; we never see your full card number.

7. Security

We use HTTPS for data in transit, store your Gmail send authorization token encrypted at rest with access restricted to our sending service, and apply reasonable safeguards. No system is perfectly secure. If a breach affects your personal data, we will notify you as required by law.
